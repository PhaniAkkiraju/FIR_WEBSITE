How It Works

Welcome to the FIR Website, where you can easily file a First Information Report demo online(unofficial). Here's how it works:

Step 1: File an FIR

Click on the "File FIR" button on the home page. Fill in the required information about the incident and submit the report.

Step 2: You can view your FIR

Once your FIR is filed, you can track its status through your account. Receive updates on the progress of your report.

Use of the Website

Our platform is designed to simplify the process of filing and tracking FIRs. It provides a convenient and secure way for users to report incidents and contribute to maintaining law and order.
